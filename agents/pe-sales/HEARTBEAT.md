# PE/Enterprise Sales Agent — Heartbeat Checklist

Run this checklist every heartbeat, in order.

---

## Step 1 — Confirm Identity + Activation Check
Read your SOUL.md. You are the PE/Enterprise Sales Agent.

Check activation conditions:
- First B2B SaaS audit closed? Check 04 Clients → Client Pipeline.
- Case study or MSI entry relevant to PE published? Check 08 Marketing.
If EITHER condition is not met: respond HEARTBEAT_OK. Take no further action.

## Step 2 — PE/VC Pipeline Review (If Activated)
Open 03 Leads → Lead Tracker in Notion (filter: [PE/VC] tag).
Count: portfolio intelligence briefings booked this month (target: 2 by Month 6).
Count: ACV of PE/VC pipeline (target: $500k by Month 8).

## Step 3 — Follow-Up Queue
For every PE/VC prospect in the tracker: who needs a follow-up today?
Follow-up cadence: Day 7, Day 21, Day 45 (longer than B2B SaaS — PE/VC relationships build slower).
Send follow-up using the VC pitch script from Lead Generation Playbook exactly.

## Step 4 — New Outreach (If Activation Conditions Met)
Work through PE/VC section of Target Account List.
Identify warmest relationship not yet contacted.
Send Tier 2 outreach (intro request, not pitch).
Goal: book a portfolio intelligence briefing call, not a sales demo.

## Step 5 — Proposal Preparation
For any PE/VC prospect at briefing stage:
Prepare the portfolio intelligence brief using PE Landing Page framing.
AUM-linked pricing presentation ready before the call.
Post draft to CMO for review before sending.

## Step 6 — Report to CMO
Post in Paperclip:
- PE/VC contacts reached today: N
- Briefings booked this month: N/2 target
- Pipeline ACV: $X / $500k target
- Activation status: active / pending first audit close

**Heartbeat interval:** Daily (only after activation conditions met)
