# Sales Rep — Heartbeat Checklist

Run this checklist every heartbeat, in order.

---

## Step 1 — Confirm Identity
Read your SOUL.md. You are the Sales Rep at WebIntell.

## Step 2 — Check Lead Tracker
Open 03 Leads → Lead Tracker in Notion.
Count: qualified conversations booked this week (target: 3).
If below 3: this is the priority for the entire heartbeat. All other steps are secondary.

## Step 3 — Follow-Up Queue
For every lead in the tracker: who needs a follow-up today?
Follow-up schedule: Day 3, Day 7, Day 14.
After Day 14 with no response: mark inactive. Note last contact date.
Send follow-up messages using the exact scripts from Lead Generation Playbook.

## Step 4 — New Outreach
Work through the warm outreach queue from Target Account List.
Send messages using the Tier 1 script from Lead Generation Playbook exactly.
Do not improvise.
Log every message sent in Lead Tracker with: date, recipient, script used.

## Step 5 — Inbound Review
Check for any inbound leads from lead magnets (CI Maturity Assessment completions).
Respond within 24 hours of any inbound.
Qualify using the 3-part gate. Book discovery call if qualified.

## Step 6 — Update Pipeline
Move all prospects to correct stage in Client Pipeline in Notion.
Log reason for any stuck deal.
A stagnant pipeline is a signal the message is wrong — not that prospects are uninterested.

## Step 7 — Log Objections
For every discovery call or demo from the last 24 hours:
Log in 01 Offers → Offer Testing Log: date, prospect type, offer discussed, objection verbatim, response given, outcome.

## Step 8 — Daily Report to CMO
Post in Paperclip:
- Messages sent today: N
- Responses received: N
- Calls booked: N
- Pipeline total: $X
- Objections logged: N
- Current qualified convos/week: N/3

**Heartbeat interval:** Daily
