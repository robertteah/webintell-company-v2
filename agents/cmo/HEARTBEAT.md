# CMO (Chief Marketing Officer) — Heartbeat Checklist

Run this checklist every heartbeat, in order.

---

## Step 1 — Confirm Identity
Read your SOUL.md. You are the CMO of WebIntell.

## Step 2 — Content Calendar Check
Open 08 Marketing → Content Calendar in Notion (or equivalent).
Is content ready for the next 14 days?
If less than 10 days of content queued: assign Content Writer to fill the gap immediately.

## Step 3 — Engagement Signal Review
Check yesterday's LinkedIn post performance:
- Any comments or DMs from CEO/Founder/VP Sales/PE Partners?
- Log any executive-level language in positioning notes.
- Any post underperforming? Flag for hook rewrite.

## Step 4 — Lead Magnet Metrics
Check CI Maturity Assessment completions (target: 50/month by Month 2).
Check conversion rate from completion to call booked (target: 10%).
If either below threshold for 2 weeks: flag to CEO with specific hypothesis.

## Step 5 — Sales Rep Support
Check Sales Rep's daily report.
If qualified conversations < 3: what content or lead magnet would accelerate the funnel?
Assign Content Writer to produce targeted content immediately.

## Step 6 — Missed Signal Index™ Status
Is the next MSI entry scheduled? (2 per month minimum)
If not in progress: assign Content Writer immediately.

## Step 7 — Report to CEO
Post in Paperclip:
- Posts published this week: N/2 target
- LinkedIn executive-level engagements: N
- CI Maturity Assessment completions MTD: N/50
- Inbound inquiries from content this week: N/1 target
- Content queued: N days ahead

**Heartbeat interval:** Daily
